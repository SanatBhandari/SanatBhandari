About Me

My name is Sanat Bhandari. Originally from India, I just graduated with a B.S. (cum laude) in Computer Science and Mathematics from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I am excited as I am about to start my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in Fall 2020.

My research interests lie in the utilization of deep learning for computational visualization and biomedical imaging. I am also interested in the application of machine learning to various marketing problems.

In addition to my research interests, I am interested in technology and entrepreneurship, digital marketing, search engine optimization, economics, and foreign policy analysis.
